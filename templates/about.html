About Our Disease Detection Technology

Learn how our deep learning model works to detect diseases from medical images.

Our Technology

MediScan AI leverages deep learning neural networks to analyze chest X-rays and other medical images. The model is trained on thousands of curated images to identify disease indicators such as COVID-19, Pneumonia, and Tuberculosis.

At the core is a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), capable of learning to identify subtle visual features associated with medical conditions, delivering accurate predictions from complex image data.

How It Works

  • 1. Image Preprocessing: Uploaded images are resized and normalized to match training conditions.
  • 2. Feature Extraction: The CNN detects visual patterns that may correlate with disease.
  • 3. Classification: Based on extracted features, the system identifies the most likely diagnosis.
  • 4. Result Generation: A clear prediction and confidence score is returned to the user.
Accuracy & Limitations:
This tool is intended as a diagnostic aid for healthcare professionals and should not replace medical judgment. Always consult a physician for diagnosis and treatment.
